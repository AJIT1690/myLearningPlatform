Is a SMSF right for me?

Using a self-managed super fund (SMSF) may suit some people as a way of saving for retirement but it’s not necessarily the right choice for everyone.

A key point of difference between other super funds (like a retail or an industry super fund) and a self-managed super fund is that each member of a self-managed super fund is also a trustee or a Director/Shareholder of a Corporate Trustee Company.
